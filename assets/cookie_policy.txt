THISECURE COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how THISECURE ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser or application. They are widely used to make services work properly, improve user experience, and remember preferences.

2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

The Service primarily operates as a mobile and desktop application and uses minimal cookies. We classify cookies as follows:

2.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Technical Cookies)

These cookies are essential for the Service to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name             | Purpose                                      | Duration
------------------------|----------------------------------------------|----------
auth_token              | Authentication session management            | 24 hours
refresh_token           | Token renewal without re-login              | 7 days
language_preference     | Remember your selected language              | 90 days
theme_preference        | Remember your UI theme (light/dark)         | 90 days

2.2. Preference Cookies (Functional Cookies)

These cookies remember choices you make to provide a personalized experience.

Cookie Name             | Purpose                                      | Duration
------------------------|----------------------------------------------|----------
onboarding_completed   | Track onboarding flow completion             | Persistent
autofill_prompt_shown  | Remember if autofill prompt was shown        | Persistent
app_open_count         | Track application launch count               | Session

2.3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies. The Service does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media tracking pixels.

When you choose to sign in via OAuth2 providers (Google, GitHub, Microsoft), those providers may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

Since this Service is primarily a mobile application, cookie management depends on your platform:

- iOS/macOS: Application settings may allow you to reset identifiers. System-level cookie management is available in Safari settings.
- Android: Cookie settings can be managed through your browser or WebView settings.
- Desktop: Your browser settings allow you to view, block, or delete cookies.

4. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be notified via the application or on our website.

5. CONTACT

For questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at privacy@thisjowi.uk.

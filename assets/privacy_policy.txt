4. DATA STORAGE AND ENCRYPTION

4.1. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

Messages exchanged between users are protected by end-to-end encryption using the X25519 key exchange protocol combined with HKDF key derivation and AES-256-GCM symmetric encryption. We cannot access, read, or decrypt the content of your messages.

4.2. Server-Side Encryption

Password vault entries and notes are encrypted at rest on our servers using AES-256-GCM with 256-bit keys. The encryption keys are separate from authentication keys and are never shared with third parties.

4.3. Data at Rest

Your data is stored across multiple secure databases:
- PostgreSQL databases for user accounts, profiles, passwords, notes, and OTP configurations
- Apache Cassandra for messages
- Redis/KeyDB for temporary session data and verification codes

4.4. Storage Locations

Our infrastructure is hosted in the European Union. Cloudflare R2 object storage (used for avatars) may process data in the United States under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

8.1. Right of Access (Art. 15)
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2. Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data through your profile settings.

8.3. Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)
You can delete your account, which will remove your personal data from our systems.

8.4. Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18)
You can request that we limit how we use your data.

8.5. Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

8.6. Right to Object (Art. 21)
You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.

8.7. Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@thisjowi.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) at www.aepd.es.

9. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- End-to-end encryption (X25519 + HKDF + AES-256-GCM)
- Server-side encryption at rest (AES-256-GCM)
- BCrypt password hashing
- JWT token authentication with HMAC-SHA256 signing
- TLS/HTTPS for all data in transit
- Rate limiting on authentication endpoints
- Account lockout after failed login attempts
- Biometric authentication option (device-level)
